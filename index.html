Restaurant Story

We started with just one charcoal grill selling halal chicken kabobs on the street. After a few yrs of selling out daily we decided to purchase a commercial building and broaden our horizons by finding some of the best and diverse cooks in town. Now we have the #1 ranked restaurant in town and we thank Allah for the success
